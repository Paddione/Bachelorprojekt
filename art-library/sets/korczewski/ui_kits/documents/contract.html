The parties wish to keep the cluster boring, the on-call hours quiet, and the runbook readable enough that a junior SRE can resolve a P2 alone at 03:00. This document records what each party will do, when, and for how much.
§01

Scope of work

The Provider will operate the cluster identified as ord-1, including its control plane, worker nodes, and the manifests checked into the Client's infra/ repository.

  • Maintain control-plane health within the SLOs defined in §03.
  • Keep the runbook current within seven calendar days of any change.
  • Triage and resolve incidents per the response window in §04.
  • Hand off all artefacts in plain text on termination — no lock-in.
§02

Term & fee

This agreement runs for twelve calendar months from the effective date and renews monthly thereafter. Either party may terminate with thirty days' written notice.

§04

On-call & response window

The Provider's on-call covers 07:00–23:00 Europe/Berlin, seven days a week. Outside this window, P1 incidents are acknowledged within thirty minutes; lower priorities follow the next business day.

The Provider may, at their sole discretion, sub-contract overnight coverage to a named partner — currently Halst Operators OÜ — listed in Appendix A.

§05

Confidentiality & data

The Provider treats all configuration, manifests, and incident detail as confidential and will not retain copies after termination beyond the period required by German tax law (§147 AO, currently ten years for invoices).

No production data is processed outside of EU regions without prior written consent. The Provider operates under SOC 2 Type II controls; the report is available on request.

§06

Liability & plain-language disclaimer

The Provider's total liability under this agreement is capped at the fees paid in the preceding twelve months. Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential losses, including lost revenue or reputational harm.

Nothing in this clause limits liability for fraud, gross negligence, or anything else German law won't permit us to exclude.

§07

Governing law

German law applies. The exclusive place of jurisdiction is the Landgericht Berlin. The English-language version of this agreement controls; a German translation is available in Appendix B for reference only.
