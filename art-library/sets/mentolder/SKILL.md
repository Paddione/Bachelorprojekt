Brand quick reference:
- **Deep navy** substrate (`--ink-900: #0b111c`), warm off-white foreground (`--fg: #eef1f3`)
- **Brass** primary accent (`--brass`, approx `#cda260`) — italic emphasis, CTAs
- **Sage** secondary accent (`--sage`) — hover, secondary CTAs, status dots
- **Newsreader Serif** for headlines and stat numerals; *italic* for emphasis
- **Geist** for body
- **Geist Mono** ALL CAPS + tracked for eyebrows, labels, stat-labels, kickers
- Voice: warm, structured, German formal "Sie", senior leadership-coaching tone
- No emoji in headlines; small line icons OK
